Noor’s Favorite Korean Drama

My top 3 Kdrama.

🎬 Vagabond (2019)

Vagabond is an action thriller drama about a man named Cha Dal-geon who loses his nephew in a mysterious plane crash. While searching for the truth, he discovers a huge corruption scandal involving powerful government officials. He teams up with a National Intelligence Service agent to uncover the conspiracy.
The series is full of action scenes, suspense, political drama, and emotional moments.

🚀 Start-Up (2020)

Start-Up is a romantic drama set in the world of technology and start-up companies. The story follows a young woman who dreams of becoming a successful entrepreneur in Korea’s “Silicon Valley.” She works with a talented but socially awkward programmer to build their company and achieve their goals.
The drama focuses on ambition, friendship, hard work, and love.

💙 Crash Landing on You (2019–2020)

Crash Landing on You is a romantic drama about a South Korean heiress who accidentally paraglides into North Korea after a storm. There, she meets a North Korean army officer who helps protect her and secretly plans her return home.
The series beautifully mixes romance, comedy, emotional moments, and political tension.
